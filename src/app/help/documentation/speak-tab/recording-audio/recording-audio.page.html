Recording audio data:

[1] The green bar indicates, that sentences 1-4 are already recorded. For blue marked sentences no audio exists.
[2] The currently active sentence is highlighted.
[3] The bar above the recording buttons shows the progress of the whole text.
[4] Use these buttons   to select another sentence. Only sentences which are already recorded or the currently active can be selected. The selection is as well possible by clicking the sentence.
[5] The microphone button starts the recording for the selected sentence. Speak as clearly as possible.

When the recording is paused it is also possible to relisten recorded sentences.
Select the sentence you want to listen to and press the play button  [1]
The recorded audio will be played.
If you want to correct the listened sentence you can rerecord the whole sentence.
For rerecording press the microphone button  [2] and the rerecording will start directly.
