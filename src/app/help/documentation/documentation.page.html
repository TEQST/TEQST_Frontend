Teqst Documentation

1. Speaker View:

1.1 Navigate to Recording:


Fig.1 Speaker View after Log in

This Page will show up after you logged in the first time (see Fig.1). An Overview of all Publishers (marked red in Fig.1), who shared a Folder with you, is presented. This list is empty, when no Folder was shared to your profile!
In this example, the publisher jannik, pub1 and Publisher1 want me to collect some audio data. To get started, you have to click once into the row of the publishers name.


Fig.2 Folders by jannik

After selecting a Publisher, you can see the Folders shared with you. The owner of the Folders(jannik) is shown at [1] in Fig. 2. You can navigate through the Folders by clicking them. If you want to go back, click the back arrow at [2] in Fig. 2.


Fig.3 Texts in folder f6_1 by Publisher jannik

After you have selected a Folder you can see the Texts([2] in Fig.3) within the Folder. Now you can choose a Text,for collecting audio data, by clicking the name of the text.
If you open a text the first time you have to give the Permission, to use your collected audio data, whether for text-to-speech, speech-recognition or both.
If you are intrested in the recording time for this Folder, click the alarm clock ([1] in Fig.3).

1.2 View your recording Time:


Fig.4 Time statistics for Folder f6_1

After clicking the alarm clock on top right ath the text overview, the time ststistics will pop up. Here you can see how much time you have spent with recording.
The first time indicates how much time you have spent with recording and rerecording texts in this folder. The second time indicates how much time of correct audio you have produced. The time is displayed in hours:minutes:seconds

1.3 Recording audio data:


Fig.5 shows the recording view. Currently the recording is paused.


[1] the green bar indicates, that sentences 1-4 are allready recorded. For red marked sentences no audio exists.
[2] The currently active Sentence is highlighted.
[3] This bar shows the progress of the whole text.
[4] Use this buttons to select another sentence. Only sentences which are allready recorded or the currently active can be selected. The selection is as well possible by clicking the sentence.
[5] The microphone button starts the recording for the selected sentence. Speak as clearly as possible.
[6] Text title.


Fig.6 shows the recording view. Currently recording sentence 6


The microphone button was clicked. The recording of sentence 6 ([4] in Fig.6) has started.
[1] The microphone button changed to the red blinking square, which indicates, that currently the tool is recording.
After you finished reading out the sentence you can press [1] or [2]. If you press [1] the current recording is saved and then the recording is paused automatically (looks like Fig.5). If you press [2] your current recording is saved and the recording of the next sentence starts immediately.
Having made a big mistake or made a long pause you can delete your current recording with the trashcan button. The recording is paused automatically ( see Fig.5).


Fig.7 shows the recording view. Recording is paused. allready recorded sentence is selceted

When the recording is paused it is also possible to relisten recorded sentences. Select the sentence you want to listen (A recording has to exist) and press [1] in the bar above the recording controll center.
The recorded audio will be palyed. If you want to correct the listened sentence you can rerecord the whole sentence. For rerecording press the microphone [2] and the rerecording will start directly.

2. Publisher View:

2.1 Create Folders:


Fig.8 Homescreen of the Publisher View

If the admin has promoted you to a Publisher a new Icon will be shown in the Tab bar. By clicking on the new publisher symbol [1], you will be redirected to an overview of all Folders you have created (This List is empty at the beginning).
The plus button [2] allows you to create new folders and texts. In a folder can exist whether subfolders or texts.
You can delete a folder by pressing the trashcan button [3] in the same row.


Fig.9 Empty Folder with creating options

Fig.9 shows an empty Folder, where a new Text [1] or a new Folder [2] can be created

2.2 Upload Texts:


Fig.10 Publisher uploading a text

After you clicked the textcreation button the upload Text pop up is shown.
[1] Enter here a name for your upload. The Speaker will see this name.
[2] Search your text in your file system and add it here. Condider that only .txt Files are accepted
[3] Choose the Language of your text.
[4] If you have a long .txt file, you can activate the automatic split in multiple texts. At [5] you can select how many sentences will be in one splitted text.
Upload your text by clicking on the create text button [6].


Fig.11 Publisher sharring a text

Now you can share your Folder to some Speaker. The Sharring Pop up is shown after you have clicked on the cloud symbol in the top right corner [1].
The allready added speakers are shown on top, below there are all other existing users.
If you want share your folder to an user, you can search for his name in the searchbar [2] and add him by clicking the green plus [3]. If you want to drawback the access, you can delete users from your folder by clicking the red minus [4].

2.3 Progess and statistics about speaker:


Fig.12 statistics view of a folder

Statistics about the Folder you are currently navigated to are shown, after you click the statistics symbol [1].
For more information about a speaker you can click his name, and a more detailed view will open.


Fig.13 Progress of single speaker

You can get detailed Information about the progress of a text for a speaker by clicking the name of the text and then select on the top right corner the speaker [1].
On the left you can see a green bar which indicates the recorded sentences. You can select single sentences and listen them by clicking the play button [2].
