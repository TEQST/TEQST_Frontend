0.2 Navigating through the folder structure

You can navigate through the folder structure by clicking on folders or texts to open them or pressing the back or home button to move up in hierarchy.

1. Speaker tab

1.1 Opening a link to a shared folder

When you click on a link that has been shared with you, the folder will be opened inside TEQST. If you are not logged in or do not have an account yet, you can still proceed and will be redirected after logging in or registering. After clicking the link and logging in it will be added to your recent folders in the speak tab.

You can now access it anytime by opening it by clicking in the speak tab.

After you have selected a folder you can see the texts within the folder. ([1] in Figure 3)
Now you can choose a text, for collecting audio data, by clicking the name of the text.
If you open a text the first time you have to give the permission, to use your collected audio data, whether for text-to-speech, speech-recognition or both.
If you are interested in the recording time for this folder, click the alarm clock ([2] in Figure 3).

1.3 Recording audio data:

[1] The green bar indicates, that sentences 1-4 are already recorded. For blue marked sentences no audio exists.
[2] The currently active sentence is highlighted.
[3] The bar above the recording buttons shows the progress of the whole text.
[4] Use these buttons   to select another sentence. Only sentences which are already recorded or the currently active can be selected. The selection is as well possible by clicking the sentence.
[5] The microphone button starts the recording for the selected sentence. Speak as clearly as possible.
[6] Text title.

The microphone button was clicked.
The recording of sentence 6 ([4] in Figure 6) has started.
[1] The microphone button changed to the red blinking square , which indicates, that you are currently recording.
After you finished reading out the sentence you can press [1] or [2]. If you press [1] the current recording is saved and then the recording is paused automatically (Figure 5).
If you press [2] instead your current recording is saved and the recording of the next sentence starts immediately.
Having made a big mistake or made a long pause you can delete your current recording with the trashcan button .
The recording is paused automatically (Figure 5).

When the recording is paused it is also possible to relisten recorded sentences.
Select the sentence you want to listen to and press the play button  [1]
The recorded audio will be played.
If you want to correct the listened sentence you can rerecord the whole sentence.
For rerecording press the microphone button  [2] and the rerecording will start directly.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Key Function

1.4 View your recording time:

After clicking the alarm clock at the top right in the text overview, the time statistics will pop up.
Here you can see how much time you have spent with recording.
The first duration indicates how much time you have spent with recording and rerecording texts in this folder.
The second duration indicates how much time of correct audio you have produced.
The time is displayed in hours:minutes:seconds

2. Listener tab

2.2 Statistics about speakers

Statistics about the folder you are currently navigated to are shown, after you click the statistics symbol [1].
For more information about a speaker you can click his name, and a more detailed view will open.

2.3 Listening to different speakers

You can get detailed information about the progress of a text for a speaker by clicking on a text and then selecting the speaker in the top right corner [1].
On the left you can see a green bar which indicates the recorded sentences.
You can select single sentences and listen them by clicking the play button  [2].

3. Publisher view:

3.2 Creating a folder:

If the admin has promoted you to a publisher a new publisher icon will be shown in the tab bar  [1].
By clicking on it, you will be redirected to an overview of all folders you have created (initially, this list is empty).
The plus button  [2] allows you to create new folders and texts.
In a folder can exist whether subfolders or texts.
You can delete a folder by pressing the trashcan button  [3] in the same row.

Figure 9 shows an empty folder, where a new text [1] or a new folder [2] can be created.

3.3 Uploading a text

After you clicked the text-creation button ([1] in Figure 9) the upload text pop up is shown.
[1] Enter here a name for your upload. The speaker will see this name.
[2] Search your text in your file system and add it here. Consider that only .txt Files are accepted
[3] Choose the language of your text.
[4] If you have a long .txt file, you can activate the automatic split in multiple texts.
[5] You can select how many sentences will be in one splitted text.
[6] Upload your text by clicking on the create text button.

3.4 Sharing a folder with speakers

To share a folder with speakers click the link button on the right side of a folder entry. This will copy it to your clipboard, so you can send it to your speakers. Once a speaker clicks on the link he will be able to record texts in the folder.

3.5 Sharing a folder with listeners

First navigate to the folder you would like to share. To permit a person to listen to the recording of others click on the ear symbol . Existing listening permissions will be listed here. Now click on the blue plus button to add a new listening permission. Here you can add one or multiple users by clicking the green plus button next to their name. Then click on the next button. You can allow the listeners to listen to speakers either by certain accents or their usernames. This can be done by switching between the tabs "by accent" and "by username". Once you are done click the finish button to confirm your choices. After that the listeners will be able to listen to the shared folder.

3.6 Deleting single folders or texts

To delete a folder or text click on the delete button next to an entry in the list and confirm your choice.

3.7 Deleting multiple folders or texts

To delete multiple folders or texts click the checkmark symbol in the top right corner. Now you have entered the selection mode. Mark / unmark folders by clicking them or check / uncheck them all by clicking the blue double checkmark button. When your selection is final click the delete button and confirm the prompt.

3.10 Downloading recordings

To access the collected data, you can easily press the download symbol  [1] on top right corner.
The download of this folder will start automatically. The download includes all audio, a stm file and a log file.

3.11 Downloading statistics

To download statistics about all your folders click the download statistics icon on the top level of the manage tab hierarchy.

4. Settings tab

4.1 Changing menu language

To change your menu language click in the settings tab on "Profile" and then on "Menu language". Select your prefered language and confirm your choice.

4.2 Updating other profile information

To update other profile information click in the settings tab on "Profile" and change an editable field. Your information will be updated. For selectable fields you may have to confirm your choice by clicking the "OK" button.
